IMPORTANCE OF BANANAS.

. Rich in Potassium:
Bananas are an excellent source of potassium, an essential mineral that helps maintain healthy blood pressure, supports bone health
facilitates muscle recovery after exercise.

Supports Weight Management:
Bananas are relatively low in calories and high in fiber, making them a great snack for those trying to manage their weight.

Can Help with PMS Symptoms:
Bananas contain vitamin B6, which can help alleviate symptoms of premenstrual syndrome (PMS), such as mood swings, bloating, and breast tenderness.

Supports Healthy Bones:
Bananas are a good source of several bone-healthy minerals, including calcium, magnesium, and potassium, which can help support bone density and reduce the risk of osteoporosis.

Supports Eye Health:
The vitamins A and C in bananas can help protect the eyes against age-related macular degeneration and cataracts.

Supports Healthy Skin:
The vitamins A, C, and E in bananas can help protect the skin against damage, promote collagen production, and reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Can Help with Anxiety:
The potassium and magnesium in bananas may help regulate mood and reduce symptoms of anxiety.
